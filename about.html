Who We Are

Alukaz is a premier provider of aluminium fabrication services in Karachi, delivering excellence since 2010.

Under the leadership of Director Syed Fahad Ali, we have established a reputation for excellence, reliability, and superior craftsmanship. We specialize in all types of fabrication work, including aluminium windows and doors of every variety—sliding, rotating, fixed—and much more for both residential and commercial spaces.

Our team employs experts involved in consulting and installation, ensuring that your project is handled with the utmost precision and care from concept to completion.

Syed Fahad Ali

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality aluminium products and services while maintaining the highest standards of customer satisfaction, innovation, and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of aluminium solutions in Pakistan, recognized for excellence, innovation, and contributing to the architectural landscape of the country.

Our Story

Alukaz was founded with a vision to provide premium quality aluminium solutions to the Pakistani market. What started as a small workshop in Korangi Industrial Area has now grown into one of the most trusted names in aluminium fabrication.

Over the years, we have completed hundreds of projects ranging from small residential windows to large commercial facades. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of architects, builders, and homeowners alike.

We are constantly updating ourselves with the latest advancements in technology and new product developments by associating ourselves with technology leaders to bring you state-of-the-art Windows, Partitions, and Aluminium Panel Claddings.
